Natural Language Processing

Enabling machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language with context awareness and semantic understanding.

Key Concepts: Tokenization, word embeddings, attention mechanisms, transformers, BERT, GPT architectures

Image Generation

Key Concepts: GANs, VAEs, diffusion models, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, style transfer

Audio & Music Generation
